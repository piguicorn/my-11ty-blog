My story

When I wrote my first lines of HTML and CSS in 2017, I got drawn by its magic, and I haven’t stopped loving them since then. I learned by myself for years and became comfortable with JavaScript (although that didn’t stop me from learning other languages, such as PHP, Bash or Python).

In 2020 I set the goal to find a junior position as a frontend developer. And against the odds, while living a global pandemic, I started to get hired as a freelance UI developer to complete different projects through Upwork.

By the end of that year, I got the chance to work with e2y on the redesign of Duni’s websites, my first project with a big impact. They valued my work enough to offer me a 35% pay rise after the first month! This was a sign I was ready to land real jobs.

In 2022 and after 2 years of experience working with different teams and clients, I could put an end to my freelance journey and joined in99 as an employee for a Frontend Developer position, where I am building forms and widgets that help clients to claim their rights in Spain.
